Skillpedia

Skillpedia is a directory of skills, instruction files containing best practices to help an agent (AI or human) accomplish a task. Everything about Skillpedia is linked from this document, including the manifesto and the FAQ.

Why?
The first web browser (1990) was also an editor. The web was designed for reading and writing, but this fundamental notion was lost over the years.
How?
Skillpedia lists skills under creative commons license, encoded in the hyperskills format, base64 in the hs parameter of a URL:
